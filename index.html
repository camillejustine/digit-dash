Welcome to Digit Dash!

In this fun game each contestant takes turn guessing for a number between 1 and 100 that the game master (Kit) has in mind. Kit will tell you if the number she is thinking of is higher or lower than your guess. You only have 10 seconds to pick a number, so act fast.

The first contestant to guess the correct number is named the winner!
